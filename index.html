OBJECTIVES

  • 1

    Skills and knowledge

    Increase public health skills and knowledge through participation and assisting in the first aid clinic.

  • 2

    Healthy Services

    Offer outreach services to the surrounding communities with public health services such as health education and screening

  • 3

    Healthy campaign

    Conduct health promotion activities through health education and health awareness campaigns at the Domasi Development College and surrounding communities.

  • 4

    Research

    Focus on research and surveillance of infectious diseases

    Main Objectives

    To implant and further the professional competencies of Public Health students at the Domasi Development College through service and educational activities related to public health.

WHAT WE DO

As a public healthy society we visit people in the community and offer them our services such as healthy talk.In addition we also do our meetings where we discuss issues concerning healthy living of people.

About Us

The DDC public health society is an organization that has been newly formed by public health students from level one up to level six. After realizing that there are so many opportunities at the Domasi Development College and the surrounding communities that can help public health students augment their classroom knowledge with relevant skills and practices, the society would like to embark on different activities to bring together all public health students of all levels
